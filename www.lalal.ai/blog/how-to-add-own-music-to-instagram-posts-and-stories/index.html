How to Add Own Music to Instagram Posts and Stories

Learn how to create and add a background music track to your Instagram Stories and posts with the help of LALAL.AI.

Background music in social media videos, and on Instagram in particular, plays an important role from a marketing standpoint. If you want to set the right tone and stand out from the competitors, you need to add your own music to your Instagram posts.

With LALAL.AI, the nextgen instrumental maker, you can extract accompaniment parts out of songs and make free background music for Instagram. Follow the steps below.

  1. Open LALAL.AI in your browser.
  2. Click Select File.  
  3. Pick a song from your library.
  4. Wait a few seconds until the processing is complete.
  5. Click the play icon (‎▶) within the Instrumental [Preview] section.

If you like what you hear, click Process the Entire Track. LALAL.AI offers free splitting of 3 songs or 10 minutes in total for logged-in users. Login using your email to claim your free package, confirm the email and get your instrumental track downloaded from the site and delivered to your inbox.

If you would like to change the audio processing intensity, click Restart. Pick a different audio filter under the Select File button, then upload the song again. Once you are happy with the results, download your instrumental and use it as a backing track in your video on social media.
