Paid Creator Packages for Higher Data Volumes

Removed track duration limit, increased upload file size, and three new creator packages to help you split more audio.

We’ve increased the upload file size and the amount of processed data per upload. As a result, the 5-minutes limit is now replaced with paid packages tailored to different user groups and the personal needs of individual users.

Upload file size increased

In just a few months in this tough year of 2020, LALAL.AI has been able to prove itself as one of the most effective AI-powered vocal removers. Over 3000 daily uploads, 70000 tracks processed last month, and — most importantly — thousands of satisfied users worldwide who extract vocals and remove instrumentals from songs with the help of our service.

We believe it’s crucial to give back to the people that support us, so we improve the service accordingly to the feedback. An increased upload limit was one of the most popular requests so far, and we are happy to oblige! Now there is no track duration limit and the maximum upload size is extended to 5GB.

Paid packages

Before deciding on a package, users can listen to previews of separated stems. If the results are satisfying, users can download full stems directly from the site or get download links sent to the email.

There are three packages: Lite, and Professional, the packs with fixed rates, and the On-Demand option.

Lite

Volume: 10 tracks (90 minutes)
Price: $10

This package is best suited for novice musicians and hobbyists, as it allows uploading up to 50GB, which will work out for making cover songs, mashup, learning to play musical instruments or sing.

Professional

Volume: 30 tracks (500 minutes)
Price: $30

The Professional package would fit professional content creators: 150GB upload size will meet the needs of almost every user, allowing to process more data. Such high volumes are sufficient for extracting movie effects, creating karaoke song packs, etc.

On-demand

If the Professional pack still is not enough, there is a “Price on-demand” option apart from a fixed package — we can elaborate on special pricing for each customer, in case even more tracks are needed to split. Those who need to process higher data volumes need to click the “Get in touch” button or contact us via live chat on our website.
