New Audio and Video Formats

The format range for both audio and video is now wider, allowing premium LALAL.AI users to upload and download files in MP3, OGG, WAV, FLAC, AVI, MP4 and MKV.

Video upload and voice/background audio extraction are exclusively available to premium users. Free users can input and output files in audio formats, MP3, OGG and WAV.

Priority Queue

Faster file processing is now a part of the premium LALAL.AI experience. When you batch upload a bunch of tracks, you no longer have to wonder which are going to be separated into vocal/instrumental stems first.

Now with the possibility to create a priority queue, you can take control over the splitting order and speed the processing up.

New Design

When you visit the LALAL.AI site and scroll down to the pricing section, the first thing you are going to notice is its new look.

Updated layout with a comprehensive list of advantages of each package ensures it’s easy to differentiate between the options and select the one that fits you.
